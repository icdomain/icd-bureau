## Disclaimer

The capability evaluations on this site compare AI and human abilities with reference to the Cognitive Taxonomy published by Google DeepMind in March 2026 (Burnell et al., 2026).

These evaluations are reference values intended to aid intuitive understanding. They do not precisely reproduce the evaluation procedures defined in the Cognitive Taxonomy.

Evaluations are inferential assessments based on publicly available information (vendor blogs, model cards, independent reviewer assessments, preprint papers, etc.) and may differ from fact.

The evaluation algorithm is subject to change without prior notice.

## References

Burnell, R., Yamamori, Y., Firat, O., et al. (2026). Measuring Progress Toward AGI: A Cognitive Framework. Google DeepMind.
